Field Observations

On April 29, 2026, a field progress inspection was conducted at Sheldon Fire Station #5. Conditions on site were overcast with partial cloud cover. Young & Sons Drywall crews were actively working across both the exterior and interior of the building.

On the exterior, YSD crews were engaged in metal stud framing and drywall sheathing installation along the building's wall faces. Exterior framing and sheathing work was progressing across multiple elevations of the main structure, with crews working at both ground level and elevated scaffold platforms to complete the exterior scope.

On the interior, YSD crews were actively performing metal stud framing, drywall hanging, and finishing work throughout multiple rooms and corridors. Partition walls were framed with steel studs in various areas, with drywall hung and first coats of joint compound applied to seams and fastener heads in sections where hanging was complete. Ceiling grid framing was also underway in the open bay areas, with the suspended metal stud grid progressing across the overhead scope.
